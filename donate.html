New Covenant World Outreach is a 501 (c) 3 non-profit organization with all donations being tax deductible. Our mission in life is to GO YE to the nations to make disciples of Jesus Christ. In so doing, we feed the hungry, clothe the poor, help with medical aide and distribute Bibles. We have families living full time in the Philippines and now Kenya Africa. We covet your prayers and give thanks to God for your generous heart in helping us reach the nations. The Plight of Starvation is here. In Kenya alone, 500 die per day of starvation. May the Lord richly bless you in your prayerful support of this ministry. Our faithful giving is what supports so many, and what you sow shall be a great blessing in the sight of the Lord.
